MSc Practicum Project · DCU 2025

Chirp Family — Investor Research & €20M Series A Strategy

Project Description

Conducted in-depth financial analysis and investor segmentation for Chirp Family as part of the DCU MSc practicum, aimed at identifying the most strategically aligned investors globally to support a €20M Series A fundraising campaign across four international regions.

Solution

Analysed 269+ investors across four global regions using Crunchbase and PitchBook, segmenting by funding stage, ticket size, and thematic alignment. Built Tableau dashboards and developed tailored financial summaries and risk frameworks for four investor profiles, with SMART metrics guiding expansion into 24 markets.

Conclusion

Delivered a data-driven investor strategy giving Chirp Family a structured roadmap for their Series A raise. Dashboards, investor profiles, and risk frameworks translated financial complexity into clear, stakeholder-ready recommendations supporting global expansion planning.
